⚡ Power Load Planning

Generator Setup

Gen 1: 25 kVA — Critical · Gen 2: 15 kVA — Entertainment

Key Rules

Never exceed 80% capacity · Fuel: 3-day supply min onsite

💧 Water & Drainage
  • Main storage: 10,000L min (2-day buffer)
  • Elevated tanks: 1,000–2,000L per wash block
  • Gravity feed from elevated tanks
  • Hot water: solar/gas at Block A
  • Pump + generator backup for pressure
  • Drainage channels dug before guest arrival
  • Soakpit or biodigester for black water
📡 Communications
  • Walkie-talkies: all department heads (mandatory)
  • Mobile in Ziro: unreliable — don't depend on it
  • 1 satellite phone or BSNL landline
  • Physical noticeboard at reception — updated 2×/day
  • Site map given to every guest at check-in
  • Separate radio channels: ops vs security
🚌 Transport
  • Pickup: Guwahati → Ziro convoy (~6 hrs)
  • Vehicle coordinator per bus
  • Luggage tagged at Guwahati, unloaded at perimeter
  • On-site: shuttle for festival venue transfers
  • Emergency vehicle access: always clear
  • No personal vehicles inside camp perimeter
🔧 Tent Equipment
  • 50 on hand — 10 more needed for 60 total
  • All tents: staked with guy ropes all sides
  • Inside: sleeping mat, linen, pillow, lantern
  • Welcome kit placed before arrival
  • Check every tent structure after night 1
  • Spare pegs + mallets in backend store
